About Me

Passionate AI researcher dedicated to advancing the frontiers of artificial intelligence through innovative research in Large Language Models, Neuro-symbolic AI, and Explainable AI.

PhD Scholar & AI Researcher

I am a dedicated PhD scholar at the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Jammu, with a strong focus on cutting-edge research in artificial intelligence. My work spans multiple domains including Large Language Models, Neuro-symbolic AI, Natural Language Processing, and Explainable AI.

With a consistent academic record including a CPI of 9.5 in my PhD program and 9.14 in my M.Tech, I have established myself as a researcher committed to excellence. My work has been published in top-tier venues including EMNLP, IJCNN, ISCAS, and ICAART, contributing to the advancement of AI technologies.
